Aicon Announces $200,000 in Seed Funding for AI Captioning Platform

The future of social media is here, as Aicon prepares to release its AI Image Captioning Platform. PORTLAND, OR, Oct 2, 2022 -- Aicon, a company that creates AI consumer and business products, announced it has closed $200,000 in seed funding. This round of financing will enable development of the company's AI captioning platform, caption[it]. "Maintaining an online social media presence can be daunting. Our first product, caption[it], simplifies the process," said Khadem Badiyan, co-founder and CEO of Aicon. "caption[it] makes writing photo captions easy. Upload a photo, select a tone–funny, philosophical, romantic–and our app generates captions befitting your photo.” "Our goal is to educate youth about the power of AI and how it can improve their lives,“ said co-founder and CTO Faizi Fafita. “We have a pipeline of AI-based platform ideas for content creators and consumers that will enhance their lives and result in the creation of more engaging content. The seed funding received will enable us to launch caption[it] and become cashflow positive.” caption[it] is expected to release on Apple and Android app stores in December 2022.

About Aicon

Aicon is an AI start-up making products targeted at consumers and small businesses with the goal of bringing the power of AI to the people and educating the world about what AI technology is capable of right now. Aicon was founded in 2022 by college dropouts Khadem Badiyan, Faizi Fifita, and Liam Betts. For more information, please visit aicon.co.
